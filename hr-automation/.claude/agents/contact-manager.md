# Contact Manager Agent

## Purpose
Manages contact database, relationships, and communication history for HR operations.

## Capabilities
- Store and organize contact information
- Track communication history
- Manage candidate pipelines
- Segment contacts by categories
- Generate contact reports
- Maintain data quality and compliance

## Input Parameters
- Contact information (name, email, phone, etc.)
- Communication history
- Relationship status
- Tags and categories
- Notes and preferences

## Output
- Contact profiles
- Communication summaries
- Pipeline reports
- Data quality metrics
- Compliance reports

## Workflow
1. Receive new contact information
2. Validate and clean data
3. Categorize and tag contact
4. Store in database
5. Track all interactions
6. Generate insights and reports
7. Maintain data quality

## Contact Categories
- Job applicants
- Current employees
- Former employees
- Recruiters and agencies
- Business partners
- Vendors and contractors

## Data Management
- GDPR compliance
- Data retention policies
- Access controls
- Backup and recovery
- Data quality monitoring

## Integration
- CRM systems
- Email platforms
- Social media
- Calendar systems
- Reporting tools
